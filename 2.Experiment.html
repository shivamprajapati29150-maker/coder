"The 2023 Men's Asia Cup, also known as Super 11 Asia Cup for sponsorship,[1] was the 16th edition of the men's Asia Cup cricket tournament. The matches were played as One Day Internationals (ODIs) with Pakistan as the official host for the 2nd time.[2][3][4][5] It was held in Pakistan and Sri Lanka, between 30 August to 17 September 2023 .[6][7] It was the first Asia Cup to be held in multiple countries, with four matches played in Pakistan and the remaining nine matches played in Sri Lanka.[8][9][3] The tournament was contested by 6 teams.[10] Sri Lanka were the defending champions.[11]"
